🚀 How to Setup the Tooltip System in Your Scene
1.Download this folder and drop it into your Unity Assets window.

2.Drag the InteractiveTooltipCanvas.prefab file from your project folder and drop it directly into your scene hierarchy. (This adds the pre-calibrated text boundaries, fonts, and the red line material automatically).

3.Click on the 3D lab object you want to make interactive, make sure it has a Collider, and attach the ToolTip.cs script to it.

4.In the Inspector for your 3D object's ToolTip script, locate the Pinned Tooltip Prefab field. Drag the nested ToolTipBox (found inside your new Canvas prefab) straight into that slot.

5. In ToolTip script, you can change the fade transition speed, label displacement heightOffset by changing Y, Locked Label displacement by changing pinnedHeight and HoverDelay as well.

6. Simple label will appear as mouse goes onto the object after Hover Delay, for locking that label use Ctrl+LeftClick, you can also drag the label if you want and if you want to cancel or unlock that just tap cross on the label.

7.Type your object's name in the Message To Show box and press Play!
